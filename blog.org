* The Great Internet Detox

Here I've finally gotten my website up and running and I immediately
want to go cold-turkey from the internet. Ironic for sure.

Let's be honest, the internet is one step forward and two steps
backward for most of us. For quite a few years now I've noticed how
the internet has totally changed me, my daily habits and routines, and
I know I'm suffering for it, i.e., what I've gotten out of it lags
behind the negative consequences.[fn:1] And so I want to "get off the
internet/screen." But of course that's not entirely possible in this
day and age---unless we live like an off-grid, offline hermit in the
woods. Hence, I want to "mainly" get off, by which I mean I will

+ only be on to do work-related things
+ one daily check only email or phone messages
+ absolutely no "social media" e.g., Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter, etc.
